How to make a great play test

1

Read the Instructions

Check out the instructions the game developers have written for you so you can help them the best

2

Start Session

Click in the "Start Session" button and accept the permissions on the popup

3

Play the Game

The best part. Play the game, enjoy it. Optionally, try to speak aloud to express your thoughts and your feelings

4

Finish the Session

When you are done playing click in the button "Finish Session" and wait for the video to be uploaded

5

Contact the developers

If you want you can send your contact to the developers so they can communicate with you

We want to become the play testing platform for all the indie devs, to help them to do great games, with your help.
